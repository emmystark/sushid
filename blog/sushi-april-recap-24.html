Welcome to the Sushi Monthly Recap! 🗞

🍣 This post will outline some of the major operational, technical, and community milestones reached in the past month. We've summarized all articles and announcements as well as some of the exciting things to come. Without further ado, let's dive into the highlights!

Automatic Tax Token Support is Live

Great news: automatic tax token support is now officially live on Sushi! Say goodbye to manual slippage adjustments - we've got all of that covered for you now!

📚 Read more about the new feature here

Trade FRAX Assets On Scroll

Frax Assets are now available on the Scroll network, and the first place you can trade them is right here with us on Sushi! Thanks to our friends over at Axelar, users can now trade Frax Assets as they would any other on Scroll!

You can begin making swaps on Scroll here

Big AMA Coming Up!

On Thursday, May 2nd (tomorrow), we will be hosting an AMA with our friends over at Layer N to discuss our much awaited perp DEX platform, Susa! Be sure to tune in and learn what all of the hype is about, as well as perhaps a small bit of alpha... you'll have to be there to find out!

You can set a reminder here

Please enjoy some highlights below, and be sure to RSVP to any upcoming events we have:

Appchain Day Hong Kong

🇭🇰 Thanks everyone for joining our keynote “From Sushi to Susa: The journey of multi-chain exploration” and exploring the benefits of how Layer N's product benefits come to life in Susa at Appchain Hong Kong!

Paris Blockchain Week

New Sushi Bond Listings

🎉 Big news for our bond enjoyooors - both $SAIL and $MKC are now live and available on Sushi Bonds!

$SAIL is the governance token of Clipper DEX, a DEX helmed by the Admiral DAO and designed for blue-chip assets. $MKC is the token of Monkey Empire, a free mobile MMO strategy game on Arbitrum.

👉 Explore all the Sushi Bonds offerings now here

Low L2 Gas Fees Courtesy of Blobs

In case you haven't heard, now is the time to transact on L2s! With insanely low gas fees thanks to the blobs integration, you can now swap freely on any of the L2s Sushi is live on for just a few cents.

📈  You can begin trading now here
